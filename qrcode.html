Scan to Open My Love Card 💕

Enter the URL of your website below,
then generate the QR code to share!

💡 How to share on your WiFi:
1. Open Command Prompt and run ipconfig
2. Find your IPv4 Address (e.g. 192.168.1.5)
3. Run: python -m http.server 8080 inside the card folder
4. Type http://YOUR_IP:8080 in the box above
5. Anyone on the same WiFi can scan and open it! 🌹
